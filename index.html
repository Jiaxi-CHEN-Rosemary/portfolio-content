Tranditional Chinese Opera, known as Xiqu in Chinese, is a form of musical theatre in China with more than 1,000 years of history. It is a great form of arts incorporated music, song and dance, martial arts, acrobatics, costume and make-up art, as well as literary art. There are over a hundred regional branches of traditional Chinese opera today. As an international Chinese student, I want to showcase some opera forms to introduce this great pearl in my culture.
